## Software Stack Decision Framework

### Current Stack (~$25K-$30K/yr)
- Compete (CRM/club software) — "far behind"
- Studio Director
- Dropbox Sign (~$500/yr)
- ClubOS
- 13 years since last conversion (2013)

### Option A: Club Automation
- **Strength:** Business analytics, bottom-line focus, retention reporting (flags at-risk members)
- **Weakness:** Less member-facing polish

### Option B: ABC Fitness
- **Strength:** Member experience focus, strong app, member-facing features
- **Weakness:** Less analytics depth
- **Treehouse lean:** ABC — member experience > retention numbers in their priority

### Migration Risks
- Billing errors hitting members (Treehouse currently very accurate)
- Last conversion was traumatic (French company, language barrier, Mike cried)
- Rebecca would lead this time (Erica did last one part-time with Luke Miller)

### Recommendation
Defer decision until Phase 1 website is live and member feedback survey (4.2) is complete. Software migration is high-risk, high-reward — don't rush it.

## Sales Process Improvements

### Tour Protocol (Reinstate)
1. Daycare takes kids immediately on arrival
2. Kids get Treehouse branded shirt → they sell parents on the club
3. Tour paints the member's ideal day: "Yoga at 9 → weights → spa → 2.5 hrs of your time"
4. Sign-in sheet tracking (used to do this)
5. Never use words "expensive" or "a lot of money"
6. Sell the problem solved, not the price

### Current Tour Stats (Concerning)
- 4 tours → 0 sales
- Sugar House mom (5 kids) — too far
- Tony — pickleball-focused, piecemealing
- Last two Friday tours: staff embarrassed by facility aesthetics

## Accountability Framework

### Weekly Friday Updates (Automated by Vision)
Every Friday, a compiled status report covering:
1. What was completed this week
2. What's planned for next week
3. Blockers and how to resolve them
4. Key metrics (if data available)

### Meeting Protocol
- Come prepared with deliverables, not discussion topics
- 15-minute standup format, not open-ended chats
- Specific assignments with deadlines
- "Here's what we did → here's what's next → here's what's blocking us"
