Where It All Started

Treehouse Athletic Club opened its doors in 1999 with a simple premise: build the gym you'd want your family to grow up in. Twenty-five years later, this 72,000 sq ft facility off I-15 at the 123rd South exit is still locally owned, still family-run, and still the standard every competitor tries to match.

Serving Draper, Sandy, Riverton, and South Jordan. Equinox charges three times more for half the space and none of the community.
