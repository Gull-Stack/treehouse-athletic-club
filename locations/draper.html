Draper Location

72,000 sq ft of world-class fitness, right in your neighborhood

Your Fitness Home in Draper

Located just off I-15 at the 123rd South exit, our Draper facility is the flagship Treehouse Athletic Club. With 72,000 square feet of premium fitness space, we offer everything from state-of-the-art gym equipment to resort-style pools and our famous 45-foot indoor rock climbing wall.

Serving the communities of Draper, Sandy, Riverton, and South Jordan, our club has been helping Utah families get fit since 1999. We're proud to be locally owned and operated, with a commitment to creating a welcoming, family-friendly environment.
