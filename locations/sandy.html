Your Fitness Home in Sandy

Our Sandy location brings the full Treehouse experience to the heart of the Salt Lake Valley. Conveniently located just off 700 East, we serve the communities of Sandy, Midvale, Murray, and Cottonwood Heights.

With all the same world-class amenities as our flagship Draper location, the Sandy club offers a complete fitness experience for the whole family. From early morning workouts to evening swim sessions, we're here when you need us.
