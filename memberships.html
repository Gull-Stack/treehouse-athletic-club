Frequently Asked

Is there a contract or commitment?

No long-term contracts. All memberships are month-to-month and can be cancelled anytime with 30 days notice.

Can I try before I buy?

Absolutely! We offer a free 7-day trial with full access to all amenities. No credit card required to start.

What ages qualify for Kids Club?

Our Kids Club accepts children ages 6 weeks to 12 years. Certified childcare staff supervise all activities.

Can I access both locations?

Yes! All membership levels include full access to both our Draper and Sandy locations.

Do you offer corporate or group rates?

Yes, we have special rates for businesses and groups of 10+. Contact us for a custom quote.
