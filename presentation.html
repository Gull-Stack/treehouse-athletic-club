Website Rebuild + AEO

Phase 1
Current Problems
  • Wix site — outdated for a 72,000 sq ft club
  • Not appearing in Google search
  • No online joining or class booking
  • No pricing visible — forces phone calls
  • No tour scheduling widget
  • SEO score: 4/10
What We Build
  • AEO-optimized — show up in AI answers
  • Online signup flow — capture Gen Z
  • Tour scheduling — digital, zero friction
  • Schema markup — LocalBusiness, FAQ
  • Location pages — Draper + Sandy
  • Mobile-first — Vercel edge deployment

Reference: Falling Waters Spa (same ownership) went from "F minus" visibility to climbing traffic after similar treatment. Down only $2K vs previously $4K decline.

AI Chatbot + Retention Engine

Phase 2

Member Retention Engine
  • Check-in analysis — flag members at 3 mo / 1 yr lapse

  • Anniversary emails — membership milestone triggers
  • Freeze/cancel reporting — prioritized action list
